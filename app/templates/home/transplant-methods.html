{{_('FUE')}}

{{_('The FUE method is known as the most advanced hair transplant technique that guarantees perfectly natural results. This hair transplant technique consists in extracting the follicular units from the donor area and then reimplanting them in the passages opened by incision beforehand in the bald areas of the head.')}}

{{_('Sapphire FUE')}}

{{_('In hair transplantation, genuine sapphire blades are used instead of the thin metal blade used in the classic FUE method when incising the channels in the receiving area.')}}

{{_('This innovation of the FUE method minimizes scab formation and helps speed up the healing process by opening up smaller pathways where the grafts will be implanted.')}}

{{_('DHI')}}

{{_('The DHI hair implant method is a more advanced non-invasive technique of the FUE method. The removal of the hair follicles and the implantation of the grafts is done one by one. The entire transplant operation is performed manually by an expert doctor.')}}

{{_('The operation is performed by hand, using a DHI hair injector which allows the surgeon to control the depth, angle and direction of the hair as it is implanted.')}}

{{_('This method is therefore performed without any incision, which leaves no scars or traces leaving a natural result.')}}

{{_('No Shave FUE')}}

{{_('It is quite possible to do a hair implant without shaving your hair. With the FUE no-shave method. This method is identical to the classic FUE method. It consists of extracting the follicular units and reimplanting them in the desired areas.')}}

{{_('This operation is particularly carried out in order to correct weak to moderate hair loss.')}}

{{_('Painless, Needle-Free')}} {{_('Procedure')}}

{{_('Local anesthesia is applied before the hair follicles are extracted from the donor area and implanted in the desired areas. Hair transplant procedures are now painless, needleless and do not compromise your comfort. The anesthesia administered by a needle can naturally cause discomfort to the patient because the active substance takes a few minutes to numb the scalp. Nowadays, the anesthesia can be performed painlessly using special instruments without needles. These tools deliver the anesthetic agent under the skin using the pressure spray mechanism. People who cannot stand the sight of needles and therefore hesitate to perform a hair transplant can easily undergo this operation using this painless, needle-free technique.')}}
